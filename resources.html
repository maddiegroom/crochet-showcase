Resources

  • Ravelry
    • My favorite website for patterns, with an extensive library and user shared projects.
  • Bella Coco Youtube channel
    • If you are a visual learner and prefer videos, Bella Coco has an impressive collection of videos. She has videos for both patterns and stitches.
  • Hobbii
    • My favorite website for buying yarn. The yarn is of good quality, vibrant, and they have a wide variety of colors and styles. I personally love the Twister line of yarn. Their yarn goes on sale often, so if you spot a style you like, I recommend waiting for a sale.
